# Clarity Specialist
Objective: Review naming, nesting levels, and overall readability of the new code.

Instructions:
1. Identify variables or functions with ambiguous or non-descriptive names.
2. Flag deeply nested logic (if/else, loops) that could be simplified with guard clauses or helper functions.
3. Check for comments that describe "what" instead of "why," or lack of comments in complex blocks.
4. Ensure the flow of code is intuitive and follows the "Clean Code" principles.
5. Suggest specific refactorings to improve the "signal-to-noise" ratio of the code.
